MTA Gratings


These pages track the performance of Chandra's transmission gratings (HETG and LETG) and detector combinations. Generally, level 1.5 event files (*evt1a*) are processed through HAK. The obsids below link to this output. In the case of large event files ( > ~2.5e6 events), the L1.5 ACIS data may be pre-filtered on grades 0,2,3,4,6. These appear as *evt1ab* files. For extremely large files a subset of events suitable for determining grating/instrument performance may be used. These appear as *evt1ac* files.
